# FLOSS Fund from Zerodha

A groundbreaking global initiative funding Free and Open Source Software

<div class="mt-8">

## Key Highlights

- **$1M per year** for free and open source projects globally
- **30 projects funded** in the first year (2024-2025)
- **Open directory model** - Projects publish funding.json manifests
- **Global reach** - Supports projects worldwide including Blender, FFmpeg, KDE, OpenStreetMap

# OASIS Network

OASIS is a network of organizations united by the vision that Free and Open-Source Software (FOSS) can empower citizen sector organizations and communities to create positive impact at scale.

<div class="mt-8">

## FOSS for Social Good

Promoting the usage of FOSS in the social sector to drive meaningful change

- Connecting social organizations with FOSS solutions
- Building capacity for FOSS adoption
- Creating sustainable social impact through technology
