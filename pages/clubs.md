# FOSS Clubs

An initiative to promote FOSS culture and collaboration among students through events, workshops, and networking on college campuses.

<div class="mt-8">

### What FOSS Clubs Offer

- Campus-based FOSS events and workshops
- Hands-on learning opportunities
- Networking with fellow students
- Building open-source projects together
- Connection to the wider FOSS community

</div>

<Callout type="tip">
Applications are open! Start or revive a FOSS Club on your campus at fossunited.org/clubs
</Callout>
